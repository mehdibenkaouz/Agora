Our Principles

  1. 1 Equal standing between users. No verification badges, no algorithmic boosts — every voice starts from the same place.
  2. 2 Dialogue before spectacle. We value the exchange of ideas over the performance of them.
  3. 3 Shared culture over empty metrics. What matters is the quality of thought, not the quantity of reactions.
  4. 4 Clarity, context, and civil disagreement. Disagree freely — but with reasons, not noise.
