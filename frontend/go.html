How to play

Place stones on intersections. Surround your opponent's stones to capture them. A group with no liberties (empty adjacent points) is captured.

Ko rule: you cannot recreate the exact previous board position.

Scoring (Area): count your stones on the board plus empty points surrounded only by your colour. White gets 6.5 komi.

Press Pass twice in a row to end the game.
