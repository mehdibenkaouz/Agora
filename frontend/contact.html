Legal and notice framework
Suggested structure for official communications, rights-holder notices, and compliance contacts.

Recommended legal notice sections

AGORÀ may publish the following legal contact channels and internal handling rules:

  • General support — for normal user assistance and platform questions;
  • Moderation / abuse reporting — for user reports, safety concerns, harassment, impersonation, or platform misuse;
  • Privacy requests — for personal-data related requests and privacy complaints;
  • Copyright / IP notices — for rights holders who believe content infringes copyright or other protected rights;
  • Legal notices — for formal communications from lawyers, regulators, courts, or authorities;
  • Business / press contact — optional, for partnership or media inquiries if you want a separate route.

Suggested text for formal notices

The following is a simple legal-facing text block you can keep on the page:

For legal notices concerning AGORÀ, including privacy requests, intellectual property complaints, moderation-related communications, or lawful official requests, please contact: Legal contact: [legal@yourdomain.com] Privacy contact: [privacy@yourdomain.com] Abuse / moderation contact: [abuse@yourdomain.com] Please include sufficient detail to identify the account, content, or issue concerned, together with any supporting information reasonably necessary to review the request.

Copyright / IP complaint guidance

If you want, you can include this rights-holder section:

If you believe content on AGORÀ infringes your copyright or other legal rights, please send a notice to [legal@yourdomain.com] containing: 1. your full name and contact details; 2. identification of the protected work or right claimed; 3. identification of the content at issue; 4. the reason for the complaint; 5. a statement that the information submitted is accurate to the best of your knowledge; 6. any other information reasonably necessary for review.

Jurisdiction and legal transparency

You may also include a basic legal statement such as:

AGORÀ is operated by [Insert legal name], established in [Insert country]. Any mandatory rights available to users under applicable law remain unaffected by the publication of this page. Additional legal details may be provided in the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Response expectations

It is usually safer to avoid promising exact response times unless you are sure you can meet them. A safer public formulation is:

AGORÀ will review incoming requests within a reasonable time, taking into account the nature, urgency, legal basis, and complexity of the request.
