How to play

The board is a 3×3 grid of small Tic-Tac-Toe boards. Win a small board to claim that square on the big board. Win 3 in a row on the big board to win the game.

The cell you pick decides which small board your opponent must play in next (highlighted in red). If that board is already won or full, your opponent may play anywhere.
