Agorà identity

A place for thought, clarity, culture, and responsible discourse.

AGORÀ is designed as a more reflective kind of social platform: a place where ideas, sources, explanations, and serious discussion can have structure, memory, and public value. It is not intended to optimize noise. It is intended to reward meaning.

What AGORÀ is
Core orientation of the platform

A civic and cultural social space

AGORÀ is built around writing, interpretation, clarification, and reasoned contribution. Instead of treating every post as equivalent noise, the platform is structured to value useful questions, meaningful clarifications, sources, and logical force.

The aim is not to remove disagreement, but to improve its quality. Users should be able to express ideas, challenge one another, and develop positions in a way that produces more understanding rather than less.

  • culture over impulse;
  • clarity over confusion;
  • sources over empty assertion;
  • responsibility over anonymity-driven abuse.

Why these pages are separate
The structure is clearer and legally safer this way

Terms are not the same as Privacy

The Terms of Service explain the rules of use: who may access the platform, what conduct is allowed, what may lead to restriction or removal, and what responsibilities users accept when joining AGORÀ.

The Privacy Policy is separate because it serves a different function: it explains what data is processed, why it is processed, on what basis it is processed, how long it may be retained, and what rights users may exercise regarding their personal data.

The Cookies / Storage Notice is also distinct. It explains browser-side storage, session mechanisms, local preferences such as theme choice, and any future analytics or device-level persistence. Finally, Contact / Legal Info gives users and authorities a stable way to identify or reach the service operator.
